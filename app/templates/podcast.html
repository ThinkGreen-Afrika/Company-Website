Our Story

As concerns about sustainability grow, numerous industries continue to contribute to harmful effects. This is not just about making the earth a safer place to live in but also about creating new destinies for sustainability. By adopting sustainable practices, companies are not only reducing emissions levels to foster a burgeoning green industry, but they are also establishing a network of green occupations in the process.

The job opportunities in these roles span across various fields such as renewable energy, agriculture, workforce development, and climate change, among others, providing a new set of prospects for any pioneering young creative minds desiring to contribute to the development of a better Africa.

By developing these changemakers, ThinkGreen Afrika is not only helping to create a more sustainable planet but one that is combining eco-friendliness with economic development. Nevertheless, ThinkGreen Afrika's goal is to ensure that youth participate in sustainable workforce development, aligning with this global shift and positioning us as a key player in the green revolution and sustainable development.

| What we do

ThinkGreen Afrika bases its commitment on fostering the growth of underprivileged youth who are capable of spearheading their job and entrepreneurial readiness. We recruit skilled talents and professionals from all fields of work, nurture them within our resourceful community for three months, and connect them with employer partners, startups, and organizations for real-world experience by bridging the gap between unemployment and sustainability.

| About Us

ThinkGreen Afrika is cultivating a new generation of ethical environmental leaders to solve real-world problems through a mission-driven and design-thinking model. Its goal is to bridge the gap between professional workforce development and unemployment in Africa.
