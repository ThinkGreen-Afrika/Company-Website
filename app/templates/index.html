About Us

ThinkGreen Afrika is cultivating a new generation of ethical environmental leaders to solve real-world problems through a mission-driven and design-thinking model. Its goal is to bridge the gap between professional workforce development and unemployment in Africa.

Our Vision

To become the global hub for entrepreneurship,
fostering a culture of collaboration,
mentorship, and lifelong learning.

Our Approach

Our approach revolves around the belief that
sustainable development is an ethical
responsibility and a strategic imperative
for long-term sustainability.

Our Mission

To become part of a global network of green
professionals through networking and
partnerships and enhance economic
growth and sustainability.

Who We Are?

What we are:

ThinkGreen Afrika bases its commitment on fostering the growth of underprivileged youth capable of spearheading their job and entrepreneurial readiness. We recruit skilled talents and professionals from all fields, nurture them within our community for three months, and connect them with employer partners.

How We Inspire And Equip Entrepreneurs

OUR SOLUTION

We believe that the best way to grow as an entrepreneur is through shared experiences, authentic stories, and practical knowledge.

1

Unique Value

Emphasize an ideal position focusing on green jobs and sustainable development careers. This focus could attract environmentally conscious job seekers and employers looking to contribute to sustainability efforts.

2

Sustainability Integration

Our community emphasizes the importance of sustainability in every aspect of development. We integrate sustainable practices into our programs, teaching young minds how to contribute to a greener future through innovative solutions.

3

Mentorship and Guidance

We connect aspiring Talents and Entrepreneurs with experienced mentors who offer personalized guidance, helping you navigate the challenges of building and growing a business. Whether it’s advice on funding, product development, or leadership, our mentors are here to help.

4

Workforce Development Preparation

ThinkGreen Afrika prepares young individuals for the workforce by equipping them with the necessary skills and experiences. Our comprehensive development programs ensure they are job-ready, with a focus on sustainable practices and green jobs.
