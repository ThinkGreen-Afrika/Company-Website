Our Programs

The Talent Development Program (TDP)

Our comprehensive development program ensures talents are job or entrepreneurial-ready, with a focus on technology transformation, social, environmental, economic and cultural diversification and how to acquire them.

Leading Experience Accelerator  (LEA-Program)

At the core of the LEA-program, we’re champions of igniting adaptability in the workplace. We use a bottom-to-top learning approach to problem-solving and skills acquisition. 
The LEA-program significantly focuses on both soft and hard skills, transitioning digital literacy.

Reverse Mentoring & Skill Swaps (Re-MSS)

At our core, learning frameworks is a lucrative opportunity we established across employees, creating space where talent with digital and tech skills can mentor co-workers and senior staff. This fosters mutual benefit and fills immediate skills gaps within our established company culture. 

Project-Based Apprenticeships & Exchange Program

With the value of our systemic approach, we create a dynamic loop for companies, corporations and organisations to fulfill their needs and consumption, where apprentices will be assigned to specific company projects with clear objectives and deliverables. Talent will focus on hand-on learning experience and get work-done for the apprentice.
